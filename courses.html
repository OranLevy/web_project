About Our Courses

We offer a number of courses in different scopes, at the end of which the participants will leave with skills that will allow them to integrate into jobs in the night life. The course staff even offers placement for certain jobs. At the end of each of the courses the participants will receive international certificates.

Professional Bartending Course

A professional bartending course includes 11 sessions + 3 reinforcement sessions for training and is suitable for those looking for a "short and to the point" bartending course. At the end of the course you will receive an international certification certificate and the placement department will direct you to work in the best bars in your neighborhood so that you can fulfill your dream of becoming a bartender. If you are soldiers, veterans or before enlistment you have a special benefit.

Price: 860$

Comprehensive Bartending Course

A comprehensive bartending course includes 16 sessions and is suitable for those looking for an in-depth bartending course. The course is made up of a professional bartending course plus 5 external internship workshops where you will meet expert professionals - a professional day at the Carlsberg Brewery, professional training at the Barkan Winery, an advanced mixology workshop with Alof Israel, a professional day at the "Milk and Honey" whiskey distillery and an advanced coffee workshop at the roastery in Landver. At the end of the course, you will receive an international certification certificate and the placement department will direct you to work in the best bars in your neighborhood so that you can fulfill your dream of becoming a bartender. If you are soldiers, veterans or before enlistment you have a special benefit.

Price: 920$

Online bartending course

The online bartending course is intended for lovers of alcohol, cocktails and everything in between who want to learn and get to know the magic of the bar world, and know how to make special cocktails and drinks at home without leaving home. After an online bartending course you will be a much more "chic" host. Everyone will want to come visit you. Imagine how much fun it would be to invite friends to your home and also to know how to prepare interesting cocktails for them and make the social gathering more exciting, funny and relaxed.
