What is a cocktail?

A cocktail is a mixed alcoholic drink. A cocktail usually contains a distilled alcoholic beverage such as whiskey, tequila, arak, etc., mixed with sugar, sometimes in the form of pure sugar, but sometimes syrup or liqueur, and additional additives called "mixers", for example bitters and vegetable and fruit juices. The cocktails are usually prepared to order a short time before being served in bars, but there are also industrial cocktails that are sold ready-made and bottled. There are recipes recognized by the International Bartenders Association that are considered classic recipes. Alongside them, many bars serve their own original cocktails or local or modern versions of the classic recipes.
